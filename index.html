Experience the Role of a Biomedical Scientist

Biomedical Scientists work behind the scenes in NHS laboratories. They analyse patient samples, identify diseases, and provide vital information that helps doctors save lives every day.

Main Responsibilities:

  • Testing blood and other samples
  • Preparing blood for transfusions
  • Using advanced laboratory technology
  • Communicating results to healthcare teams

Key Skills Needed:

  • Attention to detail
  • Problem-solving
  • Communication
  • Teamwork
  • Interest in science
